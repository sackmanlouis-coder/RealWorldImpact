01 — The premise

Most coins never leave the chain. This one does.

Crypto is very good at moving value around inside itself. $RWI points that machinery outward: creator revenue generated by trading is converted into real donations to vetted charities — food banks, children's hospitals, hunger relief. Not someday. On a schedule, with proof.

02 — The mechanism

Three moves, repeated forever.

Trade

Every swap of $RWI on Robinhood Chain generates creator revenue automatically. No taxes on holders, no special mechanics — just volume doing work.

Donate

Revenue is claimed and sent to verified charitable organizations. Recipients are named before funds move, never after.

Prove

Every donation is published with its receipt and transaction hash. If it isn't on the ledger, it didn't happen.
